Study at Chandigarh University

Chandigarh University, established in 2009, is a vibrant and progressive university located in Mohali, Punjab, India. It has rapidly established itself as a leading institution in the region, offering a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple disciplines. CU is recognized for its strong industry collaborations, emphasis on research and innovation, and commitment to providing a holistic educational experience.

Why Choose Chandigarh University?

Top-Tier Education, Ranked #1 in North India by NIRF

Chandigarh University is committed to providing high-quality education, recognized as a top university in India. It is ranked #1 in North India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) in the 'Overall' category for 2023. Financial Aid and Scholarships to Empower Your Dreams

Chandigarh University is committed to making quality education accessible to all. It offers a wide range of financial aid options and scholarships to support students from diverse backgrounds. These include:

  • Merit-based scholarships for outstanding academic performance
  • Need-based scholarships for students with financial constraints
  • Special scholarships for women, underrepresented communities, and students with disabilities
  • Education loans in collaboration with leading banks
  • Easy installment schemes to manage tuition fees

Explore the various financial assistance programs to make your education dreams a reality at Chandigarh University.

A Thriving Campus Community That Embraces Diversity

Chandigarh University welcomes you to a vibrant and inclusive campus community that celebrates diversity and fosters a sense of belonging. Here, you'll find:

  • Students from all across India and various international backgrounds, creating a rich cultural tapestry
  • Active student clubs and organizations that cater to diverse interests and passions
  • A strong emphasis on inclusivity and respect for all individuals
  • A supportive environment that encourages personal growth and development

Become part of a community that embraces your unique talents and perspectives, where you can connect with peers, forge lifelong friendships, and celebrate your individuality.

Comprehensive Student Support to Guide Your Success

Chandigarh University is dedicated to your success beyond the classroom. It provides a wide range of student support services to ensure you have the resources and guidance you need to thrive, including:

  • Career counseling and placement cell to help you explore career options and connect with potential employers
  • Mentorship programs that connect you with experienced professionals for guidance and support
  • Academic support services such as tutoring, workshops, and study skills assistance
  • Personal counseling and wellness services to address your mental health and well-being
  • Accessibility services for students with disabilities

The university is committed to providing a holistic support system that empowers you to achieve your academic and personal goals.
